Title: Apple Inc. Q4 2023 Earnings Report

Apple Inc. reported record Q4 2023 revenue of $89.5 billion, up 1% year-over-year. iPhone revenue was $43.8 billion, Services revenue reached $22.3 billion representing 16% growth. The company returned $25 billion to shareholders through dividends and share repurchases. CEO Tim Cook noted strong performance in emerging markets, particularly India where revenue grew over 20%. Apple's Services segment continues to be a key growth driver with over 1 billion paid subscriptions across the platform. The company maintains a strong balance sheet with $162 billion in cash and marketable securities.
