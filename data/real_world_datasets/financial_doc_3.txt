Title: Tesla Inc. Q3 2023 Vehicle Deliveries and Production

Tesla Inc. delivered approximately 435,000 vehicles in Q3 2023, slightly below analyst expectations of 455,000. The company produced 430,000 vehicles during the quarter. Model 3 and Model Y comprised the majority of deliveries. Tesla's energy storage deployments reached 4.0 GWh, up 90% year-over-year. The company continues to focus on cost reduction and operational efficiency. Cybertruck production is expected to begin in late 2023. Tesla maintains its long-term delivery target of 20 million vehicles annually by 2030. The company's Supercharger network expanded to over 50,000 connectors globally.
